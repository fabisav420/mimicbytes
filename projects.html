Ein Spiel mit Spaßfaktor!

In this cozy plant-care simulation, neighbors entrust you with their beloved houseplants for all sorts of reasons. Your home becomes a growing green haven, filled with plants of all shapes and needs.
Carefully water, dust, fertilize, and protect each plant from pests. Find the perfect spot with just the right amount of light to help them thrive. Every plant is different - and every loss is felt. Your goal is to keep 20 plants simultaneously alive.
